§ 03 — Principles
/ 01

Slow looking

A short paragraph describing the principle in plain language. Two sentences is plenty.

/ 02

Open kitchen

Process in public. Show the seams, the sketches, the half-formed thoughts before they harden.

/ 03

Borrowed light

Cite generously. The best ideas always belong to a lineage; name the room you walked into.

/ 04

Quiet defaults

Restraint as a posture. Loud only when the moment earns it; otherwise, a soft shoulder.

/ 05

Fewer, finer

Pare back the pile. Three considered objects beat thirty hurried ones, every single time.

/ 06

Generous edges

Margins are mercy. Leave white space for the reader to breathe and bring their own.
