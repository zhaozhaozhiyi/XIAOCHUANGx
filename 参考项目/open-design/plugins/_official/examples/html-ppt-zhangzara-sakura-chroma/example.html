The 2026 Catalogue
Four products · spring & summer release
SC-01
BLOOM PEDAL
A tape-saturation pedal voiced after the late-70s consumer cassette decks we grew up listening to. Three knobs, one switch, one extremely warm output that turns any clean signal into something that sounds like a memory.
Hand-wired in our Matsumoto workshop, one batch at a time, with a small cream rosette stamped on the bottom plate.
FORMAT9V pedal
CHANNELSMono · TRS
CASEPowder-coat steel
PRICE¥38,000
SHIPS14 March 2026
SC-02
CHROMA DECK
A studio cassette deck reissued from our 1981 design. Quartz-locked transport, switchable bias for ferric and chrome tape, and a single oversized VU meter that still works after twenty years on the shelf.
Each unit ships with a numbered plate, a hand-cut sleeve, and a letter explaining how to wear it in slowly.
FORMATHardware deck
EDITIONLimited · 320 units
FINISHCream & brushed steel
PRICE¥184,000
SHIPS02 May 2026
SC-03
SUPER TAPE
A box of seven C-60 cassettes, each tape labelled with a colour, a season and a side. Drawn-by-hand inserts on cream stock, sealed with a single sticker bearing the date the box was packed.
Refill packs ship four times a year. Subscribers get a small note from the studio with each delivery.
FORMAT7 × C-60 set
EDITIONOpen · refilled monthly
PACKAGINGLetterpress sleeve
PRICE¥7,200 / box
SHIPS14 June 2026
SC-04
MIX CHAIR
A listening chair upholstered in cassette-loop fabric, woven from our own studio off-cuts in our Matsumoto workshop. Sells out before we finish photographing it; we keep making it anyway, slowly, one at a time.
Each chair is signed on the underside by the maker and dated to the day it left the workshop.
FORMATFurniture · 1 piece
FRAMESolid ash
UPHOLSTERYRecycled tape weave
PRICE¥420,000
SHIPS22 August 2026
