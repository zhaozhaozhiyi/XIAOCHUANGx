Studio & Salon

Eight principles, loosely held.

/ 01

Slow looking

Let readers stay with the frame long enough to absorb context.

/ 02

Open kitchen

Show process and iteration, not only polished outcomes.

/ 03

Borrowed light

Credit influences and name references with precision.

/ 04

Quiet defaults

Use restraint first; amplify only when the narrative asks for it.

/ 05

Fewer, finer

Remove noise so every element earns its place.

/ 06

Generous edges

Leave enough whitespace for breathing and interpretation.

/ 07

Hand in it

Retain small traces of craft; avoid sterile over-polish.
