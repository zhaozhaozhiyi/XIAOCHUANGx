What this fixture exercises

Restraint through warmth — every tier, one weight.

01 / canvas

Earth, not eclipse.

The canvas is warm near-black with reddish undertones — charred wood, not a clinical OLED void. Pure white is never used; text is Warm Parchment (--fg).

02 / typography

One weight carries everything.

Matter Regular at 80px hero, 24px heading, 18px body. Bold weight is forbidden; size and tracking carry the hierarchy with editorial calm.

03 / depth

Borders, not shadows.

Mist Border at 35% opacity creates ghostly containment. No glow, no glass, no gradients — depth comes from opacity shifts and warm photography, never CSS shadow.
