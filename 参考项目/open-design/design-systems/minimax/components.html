What this fixture exercises

Restraint in chrome. Color in the products.

Every component below uses var(--*) only — the only place a raw color enters is the purple-tinted brand glow on the featured card above, justified explicitly by DESIGN.md §6.

Type

Two display voices

Outfit drives display headings at weight 500 — geometric, never aggressive. DM Sans handles ~70% of UI text. Both stacks ship with PingFang SC / Noto Sans SC fallbacks so 海螺 Video aligns with English on the same baseline.

Accent

Brand blue, used sparingly

--accent: #1456f0 appears at most twice per screen — eyebrow + chip here, focus ring on the form below. Brand pink stays out of the token system entirely (logo-only per DESIGN.md §7).

Rhythm

Universal 1.5 leading

Display hero compresses to 1.10; everything else — body, headings, captions — rides on a single 1.50 line-height. That uniformity is the MiniMax signature, holding equally for Latin and CJK runs.
